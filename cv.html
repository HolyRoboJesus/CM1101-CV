CV

Skills

Besides my primary fortés of creative computing and English—as well as other languages—I take great joy in music. I play the guitar, compose, and sing; and in my sixth year at school played in two live performances, one self-organised with a band co-established by me. all the proceeds of the first were donated between two charities. Where my love of music has taken me to many iterations of the Fèis Rois, my love of writing has taken me to Moniack Mhòr, and my love of the digital film medium led ultimately to my involvement with Eden Court’s Youth Theatre; culminating in a live theatrical performance at the Ness Islands Halloween Show over two nights, repeated on a loop.

Volunteer Experience & Leadership

After seeking opportunities for growth in the digital aspect of film, I encountered by chance Youth Highland, and became deeply involved with the work of their digital media organisation, Digital Connections. Together, we created a variety of film projects for local clients; invented a crypto-currency, in the vein of Bitcoin; and eventually won the Lord Lieutenant's award for Best Youth Group in 2016. Within the organisation, I was a guru for all things Computer Arts. I created—from scratch—the flagship piece of DigiConn's portfolio: a minute-long computer-generated animation entitled "Wings", illustrating the effects of language—particularly the destructive—upon contemporary youth through long-distance communication. This, among my other work, can be viewed on my Portfolio page.
